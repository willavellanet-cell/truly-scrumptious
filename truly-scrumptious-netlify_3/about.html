Where It Started

A Passion for Good Food

Truly Scrumptious began the way the best things do — not with a business plan, but with a genuine love of cooking and a desire to share it. What started as catering for friends and family grew, event by event, into a full catering company rooted in Oxford and Oxfordshire.

We believe that food is one of the most powerful things in the world. It marks occasions, creates memories, and draws people out of their separate lives and into the same room. That belief shapes everything we do — from the way we plan menus to the way we serve on the day.

"Food at its best is generous, abundant and made with love. It pulls people into the room and keeps them at the table."

Every event we cater is different, and we wouldn't have it any other way. We take pride in getting to know our clients, understanding what they need, and creating something that feels genuinely personal — not off the shelf.

What We Believe

Our Food Philosophy

Three principles that guide every menu we write, every event we cater, and every plate we send out.

01

Generosity above all else

Great catering isn't about restriction — it's about abundance. We cook food that people can really dig into, share and enjoy. Nobody leaves one of our events hungry, and nobody feels like an afterthought.

02

Seasonal & ingredient-led

We let the best available ingredients lead the way. Seasonal produce, sourced as locally as possible, cooked simply and with confidence. The best food doesn't need to be complicated — it just needs to start with something worth cooking.

03

Personal every time

Every event is different. Every client is different. We never take a one-size-fits-all approach — we take the time to understand what you want, who your guests are, and what will make your occasion genuinely memorable.
