Terms & Conditions

Standard terms for sandwich lunches, drop-off buffets and catering events. Please read before confirming your booking.

01

Confirming Numbers

Please confirm final guest numbers at least 2 weeks in advance of your event. This allows us to source the right quantities and plan accordingly.

02

Deposit & Cancellation

Depending on the nature of the event, we may ask for a deposit of 20%. Cancellation terms will be outlined upon confirmation of your booking.

03

Payment Terms

Standard payment terms are 15 days from the date of invoice. All orders are subject to VAT at the current rate.

04

Delivery Times

Delivery for buffets is within one hour of your requested time. If you require a more specific delivery window, please let us know in advance and we will do our best to accommodate.

05

Delivery Charges

The standard delivery charge is £5 within the Oxford ring road. This may be higher depending on time of day and day of the week. Outside the ring road, the delivery charge will be calculated based on distance and timing.

Our more detailed Terms & Conditions for events are available on request. Please view our full Terms & Conditions or contact us if you have any questions prior to confirming your booking.
